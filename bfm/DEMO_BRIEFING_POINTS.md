## 📱 **DEMONSTRATION FLOW SUGGESTION:**

1. **Start with Splash** → "First impression"
2. **Welcome Screen** → "App introduction"
3. **Login/Sign Up** → "Easy access"
4. **Onboarding** → "User guidance"
5. **Home Screen** → "Main experience - show search, posts, engagement"
6. **Jobs Screen** → "Career opportunities"
7. **Events Screen** → "Networking events"
8. **Blog Screen** → "Content discovery"
9. **Profile Screen** → "User management"
10. **Settings** → "Privacy and preferences"

**Total Time: 10-15 minutes for complete walkthrough**

---

## 💡 **TIPS FOR DEMONSTRATION:**

- **Be enthusiastic** - Show excitement about the features
- **Focus on user benefits** - "Users can..." rather than "The app has..."
- **Show real interactions** - Tap buttons, scroll, search
- **Highlight unique features** - What makes BuzzBreach special
- **Address concerns** - Privacy, security, ease of use
- **End with next steps** - What's coming next, timeline
